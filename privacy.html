Mingle Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

Welcome to Mingle, the Pi-powered social networking platform. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Pi Network, we may collect:

  • Your Pi Username
  • Your Pi User ID (UID)
  • Your profile information inside Mingle

We never collect your Pi wallet passphrase, private keys, or secret recovery phrase.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide social networking features
  • Create your profile
  • Improve Mingle services
  • Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Communicate important updates

3. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Pi Network Authentication

Mingle uses Pi Network Sign In for authentication. Authentication is handled by Pi Network.
